~ ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ Moiré Plasma Effect ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ by Ruud Helderman ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ Welcome to this demonstration of the Moiré Plasma Effect. The background animation on this page is pure CSS; there is NO JavaScript involved. Best viewed on Google Chrome; or possibly another WebKit-based browser (Safari). Looks glitchy on IE and Firefox; quite a disappointment! ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ Never mind; I just wanted to prove the potential of moiré patterns. Googling around revealed little more than boring black-and-white textbook examples, so I decided to do some experimenting myself in 2015. I was surprised to find you could even use more than one color. I wonder if anyone ever seriously investigated this stuff; please drop me a line if you know of any such research. ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ Despite my age, I was never involved in the demoscene. But I wanted to keep this demo in style, hence this simple scroller. Thanks everyone, be good, play nice, don't drink and drive, blah blah, and so long. ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ ~
